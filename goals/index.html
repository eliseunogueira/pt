Ouino Calendar Information

We believe that in order to be successful in language learning, you must find a good core learning method (you've found OUINO) and numerous external sources. A core method is something that you follow in order to properly learn sentence structure, grammar, conjugation, etc. External sources is what you use to reinforce everything learned in your core method, like watching movies, reading books, talking to native speakers etc.

Each day you practice with OUINO, a badge will automatically be added to your calendar. In addition, you can also keep track of the things you do to practice beyond the program (aka external methods).

Every time you practice the language in the real world, by speaking, writing, listening, watching, or reading you can add a badge to your calendar. The more badges you have at the end of the month, the more progress you will have made.

Use the calendar to find a nice balance in your language learning schedule and tweak your goals to reach fluency in a record time.
