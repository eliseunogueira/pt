3-Step Vocabulary Builder
This preset offers a 3-stage playback feature, designed to build your vocabulary on-the-go. Once you're back in the app, you can put your knowledge to the test by tackling the exercises.
Round 1: Memorization
In this initial round, you'll be presented with an English word or phrase, followed by its French equivalent. Your primary goal is to memorize the meaning of each word.
Round 2: French-to-English
First, you'll hear the word or phrase in French. Your task is to mentally translate it into English before the correct translation is provided.
Round 3: English-to-French
Now, we switch things up. You'll first hear the phrase in English. Try to come up with the French translation in your mind before the actual French version is provided.
